Welcome to Liberton Hotel Resort

The Liberton is a luxury hotel in the heart of downtown Seattle. Surrounded by snow-capped peaks of the Olympic mountains, deep-blue waters of the of the Puget Sound, and swaths of evergreen forests encircling the city, Seattle is as beautiful as it is trendy. In the vicinity of the hotel we have the Seattle Art Museum, Seattle Ferris Wheel, Fisherman's Wharf and more. Spend the day hiking up nearby Mount Rainier or kayaking on Lake Union. Come nightfall, meet us at the Blue Arrow Bar & Grill, and let our chefs show you all the local flavours of the emerald city.

Rooms & Suites

Liberton Cozy

$299 /per night
The Liberton Cozy is a one room suite with two twin beds and an amazing view of Seattle downtown. The room includes acess to the hotel gym and free breakfast.

Liberton Suite

$449/per night
The liberton suite is a deluxe two room suite with a king bedroom. Additionaly it has a seperate living room that has a large TV as well as a foosball table. It has a breathtaking view of the olympic mountains.

Liberton Emerald Suite

$749 /per night
The Liberton Emerald Suite offers the highest tier of luxury. The Suite contains 3 rooms with a large plasma screen TV in the living room. It comes outfitted with a full kitchen and a view of the Puget Sound. We include a complimentary tour of downtown Seattle and a day trip to Olympic National Park.
